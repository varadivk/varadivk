## Problem
Procurement teams lost time in unstructured emails, scattered spreadsheets, and inconsistent quote comparisons.

## Solution
We implemented an **agentic workflow**:
- RFQ drafting assistant (templates + policy constraints)
- Vendor quote extraction + normalization
- Scoring rubric with explainable tradeoffs
- Human-in-the-loop approvals for key steps

## Security & Reliability
- Role-based access
- Action-level audit trails
- Safe tool-use boundaries
- Regression tests for agent behavior

## Impact
Teams moved from “manual chase” to **decision-ready packets** in hours, not days.
