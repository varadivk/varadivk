Agentic AI is not “chat with tools.” It’s a **system** that can plan, act, recover, and prove what happened.

## What makes an agent production-ready?
1. **Clear boundaries** (what it can/can’t do)
2. **Tool-use with least privilege**
3. **Human checkpoints** for irreversible actions
4. **Evaluation** (offline + live) for quality and regressions
5. **Auditability** (who/what/when/why)

## Security baseline
- Follow OWASP guidance for web apps and APIs
- Use least privilege and short-lived credentials
- Log agent actions and tool calls

### Recommended references
- OWASP Top 10 (security baseline)
